它能做什么 What it does

为孩子、学生或自学者量身定做的中文写字练习页。 Built for kids, students, or anyone practicing Chinese handwriting.

📝

词语整体练习 Practice whole words

输入词语或短句，字符会整体排列练习，而不是拆散成单字。 Type a word or phrase and it's traced as a unit — not broken into disconnected single characters.

拼

拼音 / 注音 Pinyin or Zhuyin

可切换拼音、注音（ㄅㄆㄇㄈ）或同时显示两者。 Switch between Hanyu Pinyin, Zhuyin/Bopomofo, or show both stacked.

简/繁

简体 / 繁體 Simplified or Traditional

一键切换字体，整份练习页自动转换。 Toggle the script and the whole worksheet converts automatically.

▦

多种格子 田字格 / 米字格 / 回宫格

可调整格子样式、大小、描红深浅，以及示范字和空白练习格的数量。 Choose the guide-grid style, cell size, trace darkness, and how many model/traced/blank repetitions to print.

💬

可自订英文解释 Editable meanings

单字会自动带出英文解释，也可以自己修改或填写。 Single characters auto-fill an English meaning — edit or add your own for any word.

📄

一键 PDF 下载 One-click PDF export

练习页会即时预览，按一下就能生成可打印的 PDF。 The sheet previews live on screen, then downloads as a print-ready PDF.

使用方法 How it works

  1. 输入词语 Type your words

    每行输入一个词或字。 Type one word or character per line — a phrase like 该起床了 is practiced together.

  2. 调整设置 Customize the settings

    选择拼音/注音、简体/繁體、格子样式与练习份数。 Pick pinyin/zhuyin, simplified/traditional, grid style, and how many repetitions.

  3. 下载 PDF Download the PDF

    预览满意后，一键生成并下载。 Once the live preview looks right, generate and download — ready to print.
